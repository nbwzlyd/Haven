Fixes SSH sessions freezing a few seconds after connecting when routed through Haven's built-in WireGuard tunnel on mobile data or other smaller-MTU networks. The tunnel now uses a conservative default MTU (1280) and honours an explicit "MTU =" line in the [Interface] section of your WireGuard config, so large packets no longer get silently dropped. On a full-size (1500 MTU) network you can set MTU = 1420 to reclaim throughput. (#232)
