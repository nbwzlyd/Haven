Terminal fixes.

- Swipe-to-scroll history now works even when a remote app has mouse reporting on (e.g. a shell or prompt that enables it). Vertical swipes scroll Haven's scrollback on the normal screen, and still forward to full-screen apps (vim/htop) on their alternate screen.
- Connection errors and warnings (like the "shell closed — is your session manager installed?" hint) now show once instead of as two stacked messages.
