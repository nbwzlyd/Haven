SMB media now works like the other backends: tap to play, stream, play in browser, open with an external app, and the media sheet (convert / trim / extract audio / contact sheet) — all reading over a Range-capable bridge instead of downloading the whole file first. Convert over SFTP streams too. SMB connections also re-establish themselves automatically when the link drops (common on mobile/WireGuard) instead of failing with "share already closed." Plus agent-driven APK installs are more robust: a tap-to-install notification that survives the app being backgrounded, no false timeouts on slow backends, and the install result surfaced in get_app_info.
