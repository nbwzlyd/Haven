Toolbar and Keys polish. Disabling a paired toolbar key (e.g. Voice or Attach) now reclaims its space instead of leaving an empty box, and re-arranged toolbar keys keep their order when you toggle another key in settings. The auto-shown Desktop key gains a Left / Right / Hidden placement setting, so you can move it out of the way or hide it entirely (#245). The Keys tab cards are more compact on tall phones: status (hardware-backed, biometric, certificate) shows as small badges in the top-right, and the per-key toggles move into the ⋮ menu (#238).
